Tourist Connect Website

The Tourist Connect Website is an information system designed to streamline and automate the process of connecting Tourist and Place Want To Visit. The system aims to provide a convenient and user-friendly platform for individuals to book a place want to visit., reducing the need for manual paper-based processes and improving efficiency for both users and administrative staff.
